# Alerts

> Dependencies: `colors.md`, `radius.md`

## Core Specs

- **Padding:** 16px
- **Radius:** 6px (base)
- **Border:** 1px
- **Heading:** 16px, medium weight (480)
- **Body:** 14px, normal weight (400), 1.6 line-height

## Variants

### Blue (Info)
- **Background:** blue-300 at 15%
- **Border:** blue-400 at 30%
- **Text:** foreground (light) / cream (dark)

### Success
- **Background:** success-soft
- **Border:** border-success at 30%
- **Text:** fg-success

### Danger
- **Background:** danger-soft
- **Border:** border-danger at 30%
- **Text:** fg-danger

### Warning
- **Background:** warning-soft
- **Border:** border-warning at 30%
- **Text:** fg-warning
